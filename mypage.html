psitacula krameri

Psitacula Eupatria

this psitacula is smaller than the kramers but the color are very similar at the krameris color, they have too a ring in his necks but only the males but is more thick and more short

Psitacula Cyanocephala

this parrots are very beautiful, they are bigger than the others psitaculas have a red chest, the wings are dark green an his head are yellow
